## 3. Page Content and Structure

### Hero
- Helplift Network logo (uploaded, use the clean transparent circular version)
- Tagline: "Inspire and Enable people to help people"
- A short, warm opening line establishing what they do in one sentence, informed by their own description: they connect the resources of donors to the needs of the community.
- **No wide-format hero photo has been provided.** Everything supplied is close-up certificate and gallery-style imagery, not suited to a stretched hero banner. Default to a clean colour-block hero built from the logo's brand colours (blue and lime green) rather than forcing an unsuitable photo. If a wider photo becomes available later (premises, team, storefront), it can slot in then.

### Impact Section, lead with this, it's the strongest material provided
Based on their 2025/26 operational infographic, but **rounded down with a plus sign**, not exact figures, since these numbers grow and an exact count goes stale fast:
- 1,600+ beneficiaries assisted
- 380+ dedicated donors
- 60+ connected organisations, 20+ volunteers
- R190,000+ worth of goods distributed
- R160,000+ value of counselling and emotional support delivered

Style these as native page content, consistent with the rest of the page's design, not a dropped-in graphic.

### The Four Pillars
Their own established framework, present as four clear sections or cards:
1. **Linking Givers with Receivers**, a managed voucher programme directly connecting donor resources to those who need them. No supporting photo currently available, use a clean icon-based treatment unless one is supplied later.
2. **Affordable Charity Stores**, dignified, low-cost retail access to essential items. No supporting photo currently available, same treatment.
3. **Skills Development**, knitting, crocheting, sewing, and art training equipping participants with practical self-sustainability skills. Strong real photo material available, see Gallery below.
4. **Emotional Support**, professional counselling and emotional support sessions. No supporting photo currently available, same treatment.

### Gallery
Use the real sewing-course photos provided (certificate presentations, finished bag and blanket projects), genuinely strong, real proof, not stock imagery. **Also include the full 2025/26 operational impact infographic PNG as its own gallery item**, so the complete detailed breakdown is still viewable for anyone who wants it, without dictating the main page's visual design the way embedding it directly into the Impact Section would. Do not use any image containing the NWU (North-West University) logo or the "Global Innovative Forefront Talent" research partnership mark, exclude that branding entirely, it's a stale academic partnership reference from the old site, not part of Helplift's current identity.

### Our Story
A shorter version of their own established narrative (the Dr and Shelton story) as a supporting section beneath the impact numbers, not the headline. Keep it in their own voice, don't rewrite the emotional core of it, just tighten it for a modern page layout.

### Get Involved / Contact
Since this is an NPO, not a typical sales page, the calls to action should reflect that: ways to give, volunteer, or partner, not "buy now." A contact form (Growth's existing lead-capture pattern works here) plus the confirmed phone number and address.

### Partner/Referral Button
**Corrected: the real agent/referral system already exists on Growth**, this is not placeholder work. Enrol Helplift as a real agent through the existing system and use their genuine referral link on the page's "Partner With Us" button, the same way any other agent's link would work.
